The Clarkson Review: BMW M3 Competition

            
It makes me sad when people say that electrical cars can be fun,
        because of course they can’t. Take away the sound of internal combustion
        and the vibration and the weird torque characteristics, and you’re left
        with something that’s not even on nodding terms with the concept of fun.
